Pancake Swap Step-by-Step

Download the MetaMask extension and create a wallet. Then proceed to fund your wallet with BNB. Make sure you have enough for gas fees.

Connect your wallet

Visit Pancake Swap and connect your wallet to the dApp. Make sure you are connecting to the correct url as pankeswap.finance.

Import the Zoro contract

In order to swap Zoro you will need to add the contract to your wallet, simply click 'add token' to your wallet and copy and paste the Zoro contract, then press 'import'.

Swap BNB for ZORO 

After you have imported, simply press 'select token', select Zoro and then enter the amount of BNB you would like to swap for.
